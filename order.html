1. Order and Payment

All orders must be placed at least 24 hours before pickup/delivery. A 50% payment is compulsory to confirm your order, with the remaining balance due upon pickup. Delivery options are not available - all orders must be collected from our shop. We accept cash, credit cards, and digital payments.

2. Cancellation and Refund Policy

Orders must be cancelled at least 12 hours before the scheduled pickup time to be eligible for a refund. Cancellations made within 12 hours of pickup will not receive any refund. The 50% compulsory payment is non-refundable for late cancellations.

3. Allergies and Dietary Requirements

While we take every precaution to accommodate dietary restrictions, we cannot guarantee that our products are completely free from allergens. Our kitchen handles nuts, dairy, and gluten. Customers with severe allergies should be aware of potential cross-contamination risks.

4. Delivery and Pickup

For home delivery, we use porter online applications and all delivery charges are to be paid externally by the customer. For pickup orders, customers must collect their products between 10:00 AM and 11:00 PM. The shop closes at 11:00 PM and customers will not be able to collect their products after closing time.

5. Custom Orders

We use pure and hygienic materials for all our products. Custom orders will incur additional charges which are not bargainable. We prioritize your health and lifestyle, so please do not bargain on custom order pricing as we maintain high quality standards for your well-being.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least 24 hours in advance to ensure we have enough time to prepare your custom cake or treats.

Do you offer home delivery?

We don't provide direct home delivery, but you can use porter applications like Swiggy Genie, Dunzo, or other local delivery services to get your orders delivered.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and other digital payment methods. A 50% advance payment is required to confirm your order.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary requirements. Please mention any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order.

What if I need to cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled up to 12 hours before pickup time for a full refund. Cancellations within 12 hours are not eligible for refunds.

Do you make custom designs?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and other special occasions. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
